Sanjiv Misra takes over as Axis Bank’s non-executive chairman
MINT Mar 08 2013. 12 32 PM IST

StockQuote T 1350.05 1352.1

The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) approved the appointment of Sanjiv Misra—former secretary, department of expenditure, ministry of finance—as non-executive chairman of Axis Bank Ltd from Friday.
He takes over from Adarsh Kishore.
