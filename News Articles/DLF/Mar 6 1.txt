Realty shares jump on rate cut hopes
MINT Mar 06 2013. 11 42 AM IST

StockQuote T 271.4 270.8

Real estate shares jumped on Wednesday on rising expectations that the central bank may cut key rates at its policy meet later this month, which would in turn make home loans cheaper.

The Reserve Bank of India’s mid-quarter monetary policy review is scheduled for 19 March.

At 11.20 am, the BSE Realty index was up 5.3% at 2,051.02 points, while the 30-share S&P Sensex was trading 0.6% higher at 19,249.34 points.

Housing Development and Infrastructure Ltd (HDIL) was up 13.1 %, while DLF Ltd, gained 4.7%. Oberoi Realty Ltd, Sobha Developers Ltd, Indiabulls Real Estate Ltd and Godrej Properties Ltd were up between 2.2% and 8.9%.
